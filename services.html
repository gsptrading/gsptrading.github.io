What We Do

Commodities We Trade

We understand that every market operates differently. Our team works closely with customers to develop tailored solutions, helping them manage physical risks and optimize market opportunities. Gulfstream Petroleum Trading procures its products, including Crude Oil, Gas Oil, Jet Fuel, LNG/LPG, Diesel, and other refined petroleum products, from trusted sources such as refineries and traders, ensuring quality and reliability for our clients.

Global Presence

With a strategic presence in key markets, Gulfstream Petroleum Trading operates globally to meet the diverse demands of our clients. Whether sourcing crude oil from the Middle East or delivering refined products such as Gas Oil and Jet Fuel to customers across Europe, Asia, and the Americas, we leverage our network of affiliates and partners to ensure smooth operations and timely delivery.

Shipping & Logistics

Transporting energy products from production sites to end-users requires an efficient and secure supply chain. Shipping is a cornerstone of our operations, and we place the utmost priority on safety, coordination, and efficiency. Gulfstream Petroleum Trading, in collaboration with joint venture partners, ensures that our commodities, including Crude Oil, Gas Oil, Jet Fuel, LNG/LPG, Diesel, and other refined petroleum products, are transported safely and efficiently to their final destinations.

Long-Term Partnerships

At Gulfstream Petroleum Trading, building long-term relationships is at the heart of our business. We offer flexibility, market insight, and logistical expertise to provide customized energy solutions that drive value for all parties. By fostering trust and reliability, we help our clients thrive in a dynamic global energy market, ensuring mutually beneficial outcomes.
